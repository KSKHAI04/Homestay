SERVICE REGULATIONS

Reservations and Booking :
Guests are responsible for any damages or losses to the property caused during their stay.
All reservations require a valid credit card to secure the booking.
A deposit or pre-authorization on the credit card may be required at the time of booking.
Payment for the stay is due upon check-in. We accept debit card/Visa/Credit card.
Cancellation policies and fees apply; please refer to our cancellation policy for details.
Check-In and Check-Out :
Check-in time is after 10am and before 2pm.
Check-out time is before 12pm.
Early check-ins and late check-outs may be accommodated based on availability and may be subject to additional charges.
House Rules and Conduct :
Guests are expected to treat the homestay guests with respect.
Quiet hours are observed from 11pm to 8am.
Smoking is strictil prohibited inside the homestay.
Parties and events are not allowed unless prior arrangements have been made with the host.
Damages and Liabilities :
Guests are responsible for any damages or losses to the property caused during thier stay.
Charges for damages will be applied to the credit card provided during booking.
The homestay is not responsible for any loss of personal belongings.
Pets :
Pets are allowed on the property unless prior arrangements have been made with the homestay.
Additional fees and rules may apply for guests traveling with pets.
Security and Safety :
The homestay provides basic security measures, but guests are encouraged to take precautions and lock their room when not present.
Emergency contact information is provided in each room for guest safety.
