ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Founded with a passion for immersive travel experiences, "Thatplacela" Homestay Company has a rich history rooted in a love for exploration and cultural connection. Our journey began several years ago when a group of avid travelers came together with a shared vision, to create a platform that goes beyond conventional tourism and offers a gateway to authentic, local experiences.

Guided by this vision, "Thatplacela" was born. From its humble beginnings, our company has grown into a trusted name in the world of homestay travel. We have taken the road less traveled, seeking out hidden gems and forging meaningful relationships with local communities along the way.

Over the years, we have evolved and refined our approach, continuously seeking out unique homestays that capture the heart and soul of each destination. Our dedication to responsible and sustainable tourism has remained unwavering, as we strive to leave a positive impact on the places we visit.

As we look back on our journey, we are proud of the countless travelers who have embarked on their own adventures with "Thatplacela." Each homestay experience has been a story waiting to be told, a chapter in the book of our history that continues to be written with every new traveler we welcome into our family.

Join us on a journey through time and culture, as we invite you to be a part of our story at "Thatplacela" Homestay Company. Experience the world through the eyes of locals, connect with communities, and create memories that will shape your own personal history of exploration and discovery.

Travel is not just about the places you visit; it's about the stories you collect, the connections you make, and the moments that shape your perspective.
Through every homestay experience, we open doors to a world of authenticity and shared humanity, one enriching chapter at a time.

Harold, Founder of "Thatplacela" Homestay Company
